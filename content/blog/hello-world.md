Hi there, hello, I'm Thom. Thom Bruce. But if you're here, you probably already know that. Hey, what do you expect? It's my first blog post; introductions are in order. So, I'm Thom and this is my blog. A reboot of it anyway. I don't really know what my endgame is for it, for this site, for my whole... online persona... but I want to take a fresh approach. And so, I am starting from scratch.

A bit about me, then. I'm... a programmer, currently working with a health sector client and the NHS. I'm a father; beautiful little dude called Ethan - he's five. I want to be a writer but I haven't done enough of it in a decade to justify calling myself one. And I'm an avid gamer and cinema fan; a lot of geekdom stuff, really - comics, cartoons and novel technologies. Speaking of which, I do know that I want to focus on writing about precisely that here: novel technologies. This site is a serverless, static website on what we call the Jamstack. That's... pretty novel. Really what it is is cheap and environmentally friendly. Y'know, you don't need a server to host blog content... or to make it searchable.... It's... largely unchanging. Meanwhile, the JA in 'JAMstack' stands for JavaScript and APIs. We can take a best of both worlds approach, where blog content and search is static, but we can still add dynamic features like... I dunno, chat maybe. We can do that using JavaScript enhancement, coupled with external APIs. I mean we'll go into it in time... _I'm rambling._

I'm lost. I don't know if there's **A LOT** more to know about me or _nothing at all_. There are a lot of projects I would like to list that are in various states of development, from ideation to functional prototype, from ready for production to waiting to come out of long-hiatus. I'm not gonna do that here. And I have a bunch of hobbies and interests I could talk about, but maybe we'll get to those in time. Honestly, my mind is terribly scattered. In a way, I consider _a blog_ the first step towards bringing my thoughts under control. We've got a lot to talk about...

Hope you stick around.
