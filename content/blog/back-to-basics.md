Apologies for the swearing and fuck it, here goes...

As I write this, I have seven websites deployed on Netlify. I have another two or three hosted on GitHub Pages. And a number of these also have DNS records pointing my own custom domains in their direction. Many of them are blogs; in fact, seven of them are blogs. One of them is also supposed to be a portfolio website, but it's in an unfinished state. And the others are either documentation websites or demos. And the issue is this... That's too much; that's more than I can chew. Actually, this was a really good idea until it wasn't. I deliberately separated out my work into multiple domains in order to prove that a general framework which all of them use was fit for purpose. After quite a lot of tweaking... it is. At least, I think it is; there will always be extra kinks that need working out. Anyways, more than I can chew...

I don't wanna maintain all of that. I **do** want to write across several different topics, and I **do** want to separate those out into different domains but... _eventually_. I want to do that _eventually_. We're putting the cart before the horse here, to use a second cliché. I do know full well that the different topics I think I may want to write about won't appeal to everybody, hence the separation of scopes, but... I don't exactly have an audience to begin with; why worry about alienating an audience of zero - readers are already an alien concept here. So I'm dragging myself back to basics... Back to **one** website, one source from which I publish anything I want. Code, politics, philosophy, writing; all of it under one roof... for now. We'll worry about what that means later, we'll talk about personal brands and per-project branding and blah-blah-blah _later_.

The truth is I have a lot on my plate. And a lot more that I want to heap onto it...

**I am...** a professional software developer working with several clients.

**I am...** a father to a four year old on the autism spectrum.

**I am...** still trying to also enjoy games, film and other media I love.

**I want to...** write several works of fiction.

**I want to...** publish and manage my own software platforms.

**I want to...** produce a few video games.

There's a lot here to think about, a lot here to manage, and I am barely scratching the surface. Life... is pretty fucking complicated. So let's simplify.

I intend to remove the six other websites hosted on Netlify, with the exception of this one - _thombruce.com_. I intend to move all of my blogging to this domain, including those old articles. This will break hyperlinks I've shared previously, but I don't care (they don't get a lot of traffic anyway; it doesn't effect me much). Then I intend to work the problems I have, one by one or as necessary and just... get it done. Priority numero uno is always my four year old, turning five soon, then I have to apportion time to everything else... one by one... until they're done.

Step one: Rebuild thombruce.com to host its own blog content and transfer the rest of my content to it.

Reconvene when that is done.
