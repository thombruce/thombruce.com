Hi there, hello! Welcome to my journal. This is... a section of my website that I can dedicate to just... _the worst_ writing! I mean just low-effort, stream of consciousness riffing on whatever the heck I want. Think of it like Twitter or a Facebook wall, but... without friends and followers, so without any obligation to appeal to anybody but myself. I could literally tell you what I had for breakfast, influencer-style! ... It was corn flakes... with milk, not too much... and a cup of tea with a similar approach to dairy. ... Yay, how dreadfully boring!

Okay, in seriousness, I've put this little section together as a place to riff; like writing jam sessions. The writing doesn't need to be high quality, well researched or edited; it can be loose, stream of consciousness and entirely opinion based. It can also be strict, structured and cited, and informative if I feel like writing something like that which does not fit anywhere else.

Essentially, the journal is my place for everything - everything that doesn't fit elsewhere anyways.

As I do flesh out my website here a little more, we're gonna see stricter, better written articles appear in a blog or articles section, and we're gonna see code popping up in a projects and work section. And as I go, I am gonna sorta document the process here. This is also a dev diary of a sort.

So hi, hello... welcome to my journal. I have nothing more to add right now. 😅
