One reason this journal exists? Ultra-short posts like this one that consist of nothing more than a date (for sorting purposes) and a tiny amount of content.

Like I said...

> Think of it like Twitter or a Facebook wall

This post has no title, no image... no metadata at all, except a date. Apart from that, it's the text you can see... and "2" for a filename. It needs to have a filename because I'm writing it as a static site, and that filename also doubles as the posts 'slug', which is the path it will exist at on the website. _Look, I'm... over-explaining. Point is..._

This is a minimal journal post:

- A filename (in this case "2" - it exists at `content/journal/2.md` in my project files)
- A date (UTC format: `2021-11-15T02:10:20Z` - used for sorting by creation date)
- Some content (a little bit of Markdown-enhanced text in this case, but it could be an image, a widget, a map, anything)

In fact the one thing that is missing that would be crucial to a blog post is just a title. But again... I'm creating this space to ramble and riff and share off-topic or informal dialogue, essentially. My blog, when I do boot those efforts back up, will be more structured, more formal and better organised... which I realise in retrospect are three synonyms... but here, I don't care.
